# Leitmotif & Use Policy

## Purpose

Establish and maintain a consistent leitmotif palette that enhances narrative coherence without telegraphing spoilers through recurring musical or sonic themes.

## Core Principles

- **Style Alignment**: Leitmotifs must harmonize with Style Lead's register and tone
- **Spoiler Hygiene**: Recurring themes must not hint at hidden allegiances, twists, or internal state
- **Narrative Support**: Motifs should reinforce themes without revealing mechanics
- **Diegetic Compatibility**: Leitmotifs support in-world delivery, never meta hints

## Steps

1. **Coordinate with Style Lead**: Review register map and tonal palette
2. **Define Leitmotif Palette**: Establish recurring musical/sonic themes
3. **Document Use Policy**: Record which themes apply to which narrative contexts
4. **Establish Spoiler Boundaries**: Explicitly note what leitmotifs must NOT signal:
   - Hidden character allegiances
   - Future plot twists
   - Internal game state or flags
   - Gate logic or codewords
5. **Review Existing Plans**: Audit audio plans for leitmotif consistency and spoiler safety
6. **Provide Producer Guidance**: Share leitmotif palette and application notes
7. **Monitor Consistency**: Review rendered cues for adherence to use policy

## Outputs

- **Leitmotif Palette**: Documented set of recurring musical/sonic themes
- **Use Policy**: Guidelines on when and how to use specific leitmotifs
- **Spoiler Boundaries**: Explicit list of what themes must not signal
- **Style Coordination Notes**: Alignment records with Style Lead
- **Consistency Audits**: Reviews of audio work for leitmotif adherence

## Quality Checks

- Leitmotif palette aligns with Style Lead's register and tone
- No leitmotif-as-spoiler patterns (signaling hidden information)
- Themes consistently applied across all audio cues
- Use policy prevents accidental foreshadowing via music
- Documentation enables consistent application by Audio Producer
- Changes to palette coordinated with Style Lead
